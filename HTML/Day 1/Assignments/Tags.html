Mathematical & Chemical Formulas

Mathematical Formulas

1. Area of Circle = π r2

2. Circumference = 2 π r

3. Area of Rectangle = l × b

4. Perimeter of Rectangle = 2(l + b)

5. Pythagoras Theorem = a2 + b2 = c2

6. Simple Interest = (P × R × T) / 100

7. Quadratic Formula = (-b ± √(b2 - 4ac)) / 2a

8. Speed = Distance / Time

9. Volume of Cube = a3

10. Average = Sum / Number
